About

I represent many women from Syracuse and cities similar to ours. I've worked as a Software Trainer, Analyst, Substitute Teacher, Mental Health Therapy Aide, Zumba Instructor and have worn many hats within the customer service and health sectors. No matter what position I'm hired for, I find myself in the roles that help others most. I've worked with everyone from a supersmart and energetic 4 year old to a sharp 94 year old grandma with a social life and wardrobe that rivals any Gen Z'er.

Through all of my experiences my greatest accomplishments are my daughters. They are super talented, insanely intelligent and have taught me lessons that I could have never learned within the workforce or classroom. I take every opportunity to bring them on this journey and continue to show them that there are no ceilings.

I have now been given the opportunity to move around and work within the tech industry to continue to learn, complete new projects, work with new people and help to bridge the gap for my city and those like it. I am extremly Excited! I am extremely Humbled. I am extremely Blessed!
